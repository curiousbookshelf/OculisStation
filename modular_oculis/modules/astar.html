A* Pathfinding / Navigation

Module ID: ASTAR

Description:

Implements A* pathfinding, and reworks the Navigate verb/button to use it.

A* works across Z-levels (it can go up/down stairs), and can also "prefer" specific areas and tiles, i.e preferring to go through hallways rather than maints.
